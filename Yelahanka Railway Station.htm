Restless Souls of the Station

Aren't railway stations a really spooky place at night, especially if its not one of those major junctions, but the tiny platforms acting as pitstops for local trains, mostly. Well, the tale that revolves around this place is creepy. It's believed that a few years after this station was set up, a carrier of one of the trains caught fire, and a lot of families, and people passed away instantly. No one till date knows why it happened, but apparently the souls still seek answers from the awaiting passengers at night. There have been reporting's, by a lot of wagon masters also, of a woman draped in a saree standing on the tracks, in the middle of the night. There have been feelings of uneasiness sensed by passengers waiting on the platform, an eerie feeling especially around 2-3 am. A few people have also heard screams, mostly of children, screaming and crying for help.
